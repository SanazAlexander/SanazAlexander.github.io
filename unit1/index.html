Sanaz is the Chief Product Officer at Softprofiles where she is responsible for translating strategic company goals into deliverable products. Sanaz has also served as the Director of Product at Tapiture, a social discovery and e-commerce platform and Lead Product Manager at Zynga where she worked across game studios to build mobile features for the social gaming leader’s platform of over 300 million users. Prior to Zynga, Sanaz was a technology and media investment banker, advising Fortune 500 executives on public and private transactions totaling over $2 billion. During her tenure on Wall St. Sanaz worked at Lehman Brothers, Guggenheim Partners, Highland Capital and William Blair. Sanaz graduated Magna Cum Laude with a B.A. in Economics from the University of California at Berkeley and received his M.B.A. from the University of Chicago, Booth School of Business where she was the Herman Fellow for Women in Entrepreneurship.
